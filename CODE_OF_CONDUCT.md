# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the Cairn community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment: demonstrating empathy and kindness,
respecting differing opinions and experiences, giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback,
and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and publishing
others' private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project
maintainers via the repository's issue tracker or direct contact. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.

For the full text, see https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.
